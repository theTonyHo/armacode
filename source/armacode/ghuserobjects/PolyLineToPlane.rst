PolyLineToPlane |icon| 
-----------------------

Generates X,Y, Z Vectors and Plane from Polyline.
The polyline must have 2 perpendicular Segments and 3 vertices.

X Dir: Point 0 to Point 1
Y Dir: Point 1 to Point 2
