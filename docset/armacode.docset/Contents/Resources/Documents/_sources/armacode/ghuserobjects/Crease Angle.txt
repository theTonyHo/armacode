Crease Angle |icon| 
------------

Brep Edge Crease Angle.
Compute the crease angle between two faces of a brep at a point on the common edge.
Angle is calculated from the perpendicular tangent on the two adjacent faces projected on the evaluated perpendicular frame on the edge.
