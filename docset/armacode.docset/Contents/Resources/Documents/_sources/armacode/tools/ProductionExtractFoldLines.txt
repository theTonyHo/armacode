ProductionExtractFoldLines
--------------------------
Extract edges from a Polysurface.,
Add to document in corresponding layer per edge type.

.. rubric:: Notes

Edge Types:

* CUT
* FOLD_VALLEY
* FOLD_MOUNTAIN
* ENGRAVE
