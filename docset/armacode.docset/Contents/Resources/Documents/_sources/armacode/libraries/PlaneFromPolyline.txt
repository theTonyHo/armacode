PlaneFromPolyline
-----------------

.. py:Function:: PlaneFromPolyline(pline)


Calculate a plane from a polyline.
A Polyline Curve must have exactly 3 points.
The plane origin at middle point and x-axis aligned to the end point. X-Axis aligned with the tangent direction of the polyline.
The motion of writing a capital 'L'.

:param pline: Polyline curve


:returns: Plane on success
