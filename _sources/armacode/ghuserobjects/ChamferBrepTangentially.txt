ChamferBrepTangentially |icon| 
-------------------------------

Chamfers the edges of a Polyhedron using the Tangentail Chamfer Method.

Notes:
- Brep must be closed
- Edges of Brep must be straight
- Works on Single Breps, Lists and Grafted Lists
